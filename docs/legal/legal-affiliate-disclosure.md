## Short version

WhereToStayEurope participates in affiliate programs. When you click a link on this site and make a booking or purchase on the partner site, we may earn a commission. **You pay the same price.** This is how the site supports itself without running ads.

## What we recommend

We recommend neighborhoods, hotels, and services based on editorial judgment and reader usefulness — not on commission rate. We do not accept payment to rank any specific hotel higher, exclude competitors, or write about a property we wouldn't otherwise cover. Several of the highest-commission partners we work with (e.g., Expedia Group) have lower recommendation weight in our content than lower-commission partners (e.g., Booking.com) because of reader fit.

If a hotel has a lower-commission booking channel that is better for readers (e.g., booking direct on the hotel's own website for a better rate), we will say so — even though it costs us a commission.

## How to spot an affiliate link

Affiliate links on this site behave like normal links but carry a tracking parameter that attributes the click to us. You can spot them:

- A small label next to the call-to-action: "Affiliate link"
- The in-page disclosure block appearing above the first affiliate link on every page
- The site-wide banner at the top of every page
- The URL contains a partner tracking parameter (e.g., `aid=` for Booking.com)

## What data partners see when you click

When you click an affiliate link, the partner site learns that the click came from us (via the referrer and tracking parameter). They do not learn who you are. If you complete a booking, they know a sale came from our site so commission can be credited. Their own privacy policy governs what happens after that.

## FTC, CMA, and EU compliance statement

This page and the in-page disclosure blocks throughout the site are our "clear and conspicuous" disclosure under:

- US Federal Trade Commission (FTC) guidelines on endorsements and testimonials
- UK Competition and Markets Authority (CMA) guidance on hidden advertising
- EU Unfair Commercial Practices Directive (2005/29/EC) and equivalent national implementations

If you ever find a page with affiliate links but no disclosure, that is a bug — please email **contact@wheretostayeurope.com** and we will fix it.
