## What cookies are

Small text files stored in your browser. Some are essential for a site to work; others enable features like embedded maps or track affiliate clicks.

## What we use

### Strictly necessary (always on — no consent required under ePrivacy)

| Purpose | Source | Lifetime |
|---|---|---|
| Session authentication for admin area | First-party | Session |
| CSRF protection on forms | First-party | Session |
| Cookie consent preferences (Klaro) | First-party | 6 months |

### Optional (only set after you give consent)

| Category | Source | Purpose | Lifetime |
|---|---|---|---|
| Maps | Mapbox (mapbox.com) | Render interactive neighborhood maps | Session |
| Affiliate tracking | Booking.com, Expedia Group, Agoda, GetYourGuide | Credit clicks if you book | Up to 30 days |

Note: our **analytics (Plausible) does not use cookies** and does not collect personal data, so no consent is required for it to function.

## Third-party cookies

Some consent-gated features are provided by third parties whose cookies fall under their own privacy policies:

- Mapbox: [Mapbox Privacy Policy](https://www.mapbox.com/legal/privacy)
- Booking.com: [Booking Privacy Policy](https://www.booking.com/content/privacy.html)
- Other affiliate partners: policies linked on their respective sites

## How to manage cookies

- Click the **Cookie Preferences** link in our footer at any time to change your choices.
- Use your browser's settings to block or delete cookies (may break some features).
- On mobile, check your operating system's privacy settings for additional controls.

## What happens if you decline

- Interactive maps fall back to a static image with a note: "Click to enable map — sets cookies."
- Affiliate links still work; we still receive attribution via server-side URL parameters, but without the client-side cookie some partners use for longer attribution windows.
- All editorial content is fully accessible.
