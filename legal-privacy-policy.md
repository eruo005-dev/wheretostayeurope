## 2. What personal data we collect

We aim to collect as little as possible.

**When you visit the site:**
- Server logs (IP address, browser, pages viewed, referrer) — retained 14 days for security and abuse prevention.
- Privacy-friendly analytics via **Plausible Analytics** — no cookies, no cross-site tracking, no personal identifiers stored. Aggregated page views and referrers only.

**If you consent to optional cookies:**
- Map display (Mapbox): stores a session identifier to deliver map tiles.
- Affiliate tracking (Booking.com and others): when you click an affiliate link, the destination site may set tracking cookies — these are their cookies, governed by their privacy policies.

**If you subscribe to our email list:**
- Email address (required), language preference, country-of-interest tags.
- Optional: first name, travel-type interests.
- Opt-in confirmed via double-opt-in.

**If you contact us:**
- Email address, message content.
